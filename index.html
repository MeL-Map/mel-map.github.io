MeL Map

MeLCat is an online catalog service that allows patrons of participating libraries to borrow materials - books, movies, music, and more - from all over Michigan and have them delivered to their home library. This interactive map tracks the circulation of materials since 2006 between Wayne State University and its statewide partners.

See the accompanying LibGuide for more statistics.

Created by Wayne State University Libraries.
